Punjab Birth of Gulnaara
Story of Gulnaara

“Sat Sri Akaal”

 

Lying in the northern half, one of the most vibrant and culturally rich states of India is Punjab. The artistry here flows through generations. From basketry to pottery, durrie to parandi, the state proudly promotes its extravagant range of handicrafts. Bright and seeped in color, Punjabi jutti, or 'shoes that even the birds could wear while taking flight' as the cobblers refer to them, are a world-famous craft of the region today despite being indigenous. Not so diversely known, but crafts like toy making, which dates back to the Indus Valley Civilization, are also something Punjab celebrates. Whether it is hand printing, kite making, or painting on mud walls, Punjabis believe in invoking God's blessings and warding off the evil eye with art, craft, and beauty. 

One customary practice by women we were drawn to was making the Phulkari dupattas. As exquisite as the women who make it, Phulkari is an embroidery technique that is believed to be an artistic marvel and thus finds its unique place in every Punjabi heart, including ours. Gulnaara.com is us sharing our love of phulkari with the world.

 

 

 

 

Our journey at Gulnaara started with a Craft Research and Documentation initiative we embarked on in the third year of our college. We traveled to Punjab and documented the stories of Phulkari artisans following a three-month research period. We were not only awed by the beauty of Phulkari but also inspired by its intrinsic tales to start something that brought these timeless pieces to the notice of the people.

 

At Gulnaara.com, you will find a wide variety of Phulkari products, including dupattas, sarees, suits, jackets, stoles, and bags. Each piece is uniquely crafted with exquisite embroidery and vibrant colors, making them perfect for any occasion or style.

We are committed to providing exceptional customer service and a seamless shopping experience. From the moment you visit our website to when your order is delivered, we strive to ensure that every step of the process is smooth and hassle-free.

We believe in the power of community and giving back, so we work closely with our artisans to support their livelihoods and promote the preservation of traditional crafts. By purchasing from our website, you are not only receiving a beautiful piece of Phulkari but also supporting the artisans and their families who bring these creations to life.
