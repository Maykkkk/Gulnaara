Pehchaan- An Initiative Pehchaan
As we ventured into the beautiful journey of discovering Phulkari, we started an initiative where we documented the mesmerising process of creating these embroidered pieces in the form of an AV documentary and an elegant document which explores the immense empowerment that these women have achieved while practicing this craft.

In the scorching summers of June, as our team set out to explore what Phulkari really was all about, we heard stories that simply mesmerized our souls. From the tales of grandmothers to the glow on the face of the newly wedded bride, every memory of these women resonated with Phulkaris on a level we never heard of before. A simple girl from a little town called Nabha becomes a pioneer in the craft. Once dependent on her husband, she now not only earns but teaches hundreds of women alike. She breaks free of the barriers through the means of this craft and reaches heights she never ever dreamt of before!

 

Pehchaan is an initiative, born out of this urge to share these inspiring stories of women who have realised their identities, living their lives to its fullest with the power of a needle and thread.
